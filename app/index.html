Mission

Our mission is to guide and support gifted individuals as they identify their areas of interest, academic and/or career goals and work in partnership with them to create a map to realize their highest aspirations. In addition, we offer insight, support and resources to address the unique social and emotional challenges that gifted individuals and families face.
