Mission

Our mission is to guide and support gifted individuals in identifying their areas of interest, academic and/or career goals and work in partnership with them to create a map to realize their highest aspirations. In addition, we offer insight, support and resources to address the unique social and emotional challenges that gifted individuals and families face.

About

WiseGuide 4 GiftedLives was created by Jenny Sanders as a resource for gifted individuals and families of gifted children. Mrs. Sanders holds a Master’s degree in Gifted & Talented Education, is a member of the Texas Association of Gifted & Talented (TAGT), the National Association of Gifted Children (NAGC), and SENG, a national organization whose work focuses on supporting the emotional needs of the gifted. In addition, she is certified as a Parent Group Facilitator for the SENG organization.

Her office is conveniently located in the Clear Lake area of Houston.
